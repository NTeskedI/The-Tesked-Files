ABOUT TESKED PROJECTS

Dungeons and Dumbasses

The Dungeons and Dumbasses project is one of the first projects i ever made. Me and one of my highschool best friends decided that it would be a fun project to make a repository website for our ongoing Dungeons and Dragons campaign. The initial idea was to have it be a short project with some information about our characters as well as a timeline to keep track of sessions.
