Dungeons and Dumbasses

The Dungeons and Dumbasses project is one of the first projects i ever made. Me and one of my highschool best friends decided that it would be a fun project to make a repository website for our ongoing Dungeons and Dragons campaign. The initial idea was to have it be a short project with some information about our characters as well as a timeline to keep track of sessions.

NTI Elevkår Graphic Guideline

The NTI Elevkår is the student council of the highschool i went to. When i joined the council they did not have a proper graphic design image, rather just doing what they feel like. I decided that it would be a fun challenge to take on and create a design document for us. Me and the council decided on the colors and then i was off on my own pretty much. It was a very educational experience and i learned a lot about implementing design rules and following them.
